This is the landing page for a data visualization of global weather, based on meteorological observations from 547 weather stations around the world chosen at random. It shows each of four weather conditions plotted against latitude (between 60 degrees south and 80 degrees north):

  • temperature,
  • relative humidity,
  • cloud cover, and
  • wind speed.
It uses the xkcd mode in matplotlib.pyplot.
